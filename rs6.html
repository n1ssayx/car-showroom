Audi RS6 Avant

The Audi RS6 Avant is not just a car — it’s a statement. Designed for those who demand the perfect balance of daily usability and extreme performance, the RS6 takes the concept of a sports wagon to an entirely new level.

Under the hood, the RS6 hides a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 that delivers blistering acceleration while still maintaining refinement on long drives. With Audi’s legendary quattro all-wheel drive and adaptive air suspension, it sticks to the road with confidence, no matter the conditions.

Step inside, and you’re welcomed by a cabin that blends luxury with racing DNA. Fine leather, carbon fiber accents, RS sports seats, and cutting-edge Virtual Cockpit digital display make the driver feel connected to every aspect of the machine.

The RS6 also comes equipped with the latest Audi technology, including advanced driver assistance systems, premium sound, and customizable drive modes — allowing you to switch from comfortable cruiser to track-ready beast in seconds.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 591 hp
  • 0–100 km/h: 3.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: 305 km/h (electronically limited)
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Tiptronic
  • Drive: Quattro All-Wheel Drive
  • Suspension: Adaptive Air Suspension
  • Interior: RS Sports Seats with Valcona Leather
