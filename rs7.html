Audi RS7 Sportback

The Audi RS7 Sportback is a high-performance luxury fastback that fuses breathtaking power with everyday usability. Its aggressive, coupe-like silhouette and signature RS details make a bold statement, while the spacious interior offers comfort for five and ample cargo space.

Under the hood, the RS7 boasts a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, delivering exhilarating acceleration and a thrilling driving experience. With quattro all-wheel drive and adaptive air suspension, the RS7 offers razor-sharp handling and confidence on any road.

Inside, the RS7 surrounds you with premium materials, RS sports seats, and Audi’s advanced Virtual Cockpit. Cutting-edge technology, customizable drive modes, and a premium sound system ensure every drive is both exciting and refined.

Whether you’re cruising the highway or carving through corners, the RS7 is the perfect fusion of practicality, innovation, and raw power—designed for those who demand the extraordinary.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 591 hp
  • 0–100 km/h: 3.6 seconds
  • Top Speed: 305 km/h (electronically limited)
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Tiptronic
  • Drive: Quattro All-Wheel Drive
  • Suspension: Adaptive Air Suspension
  • Interior: RS Sports Seats with Valcona Leather
  • Body Style: 5-Door Sportback
