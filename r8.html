Audi R8

The Audi R8 is the ultimate expression of Audi’s motorsport heritage, engineered for those who crave pure driving excitement. Its mid-mounted 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine produces a spine-tingling soundtrack and delivers breathtaking acceleration.

The R8’s lightweight chassis, advanced quattro all-wheel drive, and magnetic ride suspension provide razor-sharp handling and agility, making every drive unforgettable—on both road and track.

The exterior features sculpted lines, signature LED lighting, and a purposeful stance, while the interior surrounds the driver with luxury materials, intuitive controls, and Audi’s Virtual Cockpit. Every detail is designed to connect you to the road and the car.

The R8 is not just a supercar—it’s an icon of performance, style, and innovation, offering a thrilling experience every time you take the wheel.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 5.2L Naturally Aspirated V10
  • Horsepower: 562–602 hp (depending on model)
  • 0–100 km/h: 3.2–3.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: 330 km/h
  • Transmission: 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch
  • Drive: Quattro All-Wheel Drive (RWD available on some models)
  • Chassis: Aluminum and Carbon Fiber Construction
  • Interior: Nappa Leather, Virtual Cockpit
