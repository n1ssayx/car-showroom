Audi Q7

The Audi Q7 is a premium SUV that redefines family travel with its blend of versatility, comfort, and advanced technology. Its elegant design is complemented by a spacious, three-row interior that accommodates up to seven passengers in luxury.

Under the hood, the Q7 offers powerful yet efficient engine options, including a turbocharged four-cylinder and a mild-hybrid V6. With quattro all-wheel drive and adaptive air suspension, the Q7 delivers a smooth, composed ride and confident handling in all conditions.

Step inside, and you’ll find high-quality materials, customizable ambient lighting, and the latest infotainment and driver assistance systems. The Q7’s advanced technology ensures every journey is safe, connected, and enjoyable for the whole family.

Whether it’s daily commuting, long road trips, or weekend adventures, the Q7 delivers a refined and capable driving experience for every journey.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 2.0L Turbo I4 or 3.0L V6 (with mild-hybrid)
  • Horsepower: 248–335 hp (depending on engine)
  • 0–100 km/h: 5.9–7.1 seconds
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Tiptronic
  • Drive: Quattro All-Wheel Drive
  • Seating: Up to 7 Passengers
  • Interior: Leather, Panoramic Sunroof, Advanced Infotainment
